Philly Car Wash is a leading provider of premium mobile detail services in Philadelphia. Our experienced technicians use the latest tools and techniques to ensure that your vehicle is cleaned to the highest standards. Whether you need a basic wash or a full detailing, we've got you covered.

Interior & Exterior detailing

Our auto detailing services cover both interior and exterior aspects of your vehicle, ensuring a thorough clean. From vacuuming the interiors to leather conditioning, we'll leave no detail untouched. We also clean the windows, mirrors, and other interior surfaces. On the exterior, we'll take care of the tires, rims, wheel wells, door jambs, wiper blades, and trim, bringing your car back to a like-new condition. Get ready to be amazed by the revitalized appearance of your vehicle.

Ceramic Coating

Protect your vehicle's paint with A2D Ceramic Coating. Our specialized solution, composed of chemical polymers, creates a protective layer on the exterior of your car. Blending seamlessly with your vehicle's paint, this added hydrophobic layer provides long-term protection against external damage. Experience the peace of mind that comes with knowing your car is protected with A2D Ceramic Coating.

Detailing Services

Interior Detail

  • Detail all areas inside the vehicle with compressed air, brushes, cleaners, and steam with high quality products
  • Detail and clean entire console, dashboard, and compartments
  • Vacuum entire interior including the trunk of your vehicle
  • Shampoo and extract/steam all carpets, floormats, and seats
  • Remove debree and stains from headliner
  • Sanitize and condition all plastics
  • Clean all windows

Exterior Detail

  • Pressure wash to remove dust, debree, bugs, and tar
  • Wash and clean of all wheels, wheel wells, and rims
  • Cleaning of all door jambs, and inside fuel door
  • Dry entire vehicle with forced air where needed and/or scratch free microfiber towels
  • Restore & Protect trim with no run water based dressing

Extra Services

  • Clay Bar Treatment of all painted surfaces to remove all other contaminants in the paint
  • Application of wax or polymer paint sealant
  • Wiper Replacement
  • Air Filter Replacement
